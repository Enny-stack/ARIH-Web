About ARIH

ARIH is a platform to identify high-potential African innovations, strengthen them, and connect them to partners, pilots, and funding.

Mission

Accelerate applied research and innovation that unlocks inclusive growth across Africa.

Selection

Merit-based selection with clear criteria, due diligence, and transparent timelines.

Outcomes

Support for pilots, market access, partnerships, and measurable impact reporting.

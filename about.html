About ARIH

ARIH is a platform to identify high-potential African innovations, strengthen them, and connect them to partners, pilots, and funding.

Mission

To accelerate high-impact, applied rresearch and innovation across by connecting knowledge to practice, strengthing collaboration, and enabling scalable solutions that advance inclusive development, competitiveness, and resilence.

Vision

To become Africa's leading hub for research excellence and innovation, shaping policies, industries and enterprises through data, insight, and collaboration.

Why ARIH Matters

Africa’s growth depends on its ability to generate and apply knowledge effectively. ARIH exists to ensure that African research and innovation are not only produced but utilized, scaled, and monetized, turning ideas into outcomes that benefit economies, institutions, and communities.

Our Approach

ARIH adopts a collaborative, interdisciplinary, and impact-focused approach, combining rigorous research methodologies with practical implementation strategies. By leveraging local expertise and global best practices, the Hub ensures that solutions are context-relevant, scalable, and sustainable.
